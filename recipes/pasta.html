Pasta Recipe

The very best pasta you've ever seen

Once you have this pasta you will never want to stop eating food again

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Water
  • Vinegar oil

Instructions:

  1. Put water in pot over stove and boil it
  2. Put some vinegar oil in. Just a tad, like a spoonful
  3. Put the pasta in
  4. Wait like 10 min and then test if the pasta is soft by poking it or tasting it
  5. If pasta soft, awesome. If not, better luck next time
