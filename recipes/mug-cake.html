Mug Cake

Awesome mug cake recipe

Super easy to make and tasty and yum

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour
  • White Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Dash Salt
  • Butter
  • Milk (any kind)
  • Vanilla

Instructions:

  1. Add the dry ingredients to a microwave safe mug; flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Stir in the wet ingredients: milk, melted butter, and vanilla.
  3. Microwave for 70-90 seconds.
